The Process

A ROADMAP OF PRECISION

An ordered, visual flow that moves from surface discovery to final delivery.

Stage 01

INITIAL ASSESSMENT AND PREMIUM WASH

A full walk-around inspection, foam pre-rinse, and two-bucket wash reveal the true surface before correction begins.

AssessmentFoam RinseTwo-Bucket
Stage 02

CLAY BAR APPLICATION

A lubricated clay glide removes bonded contaminants for a glass-smooth finish ready for deeper prep.

DecontaminateSmooth Surface
Stage 03

DEEP CLEANING AND SURFACE PREPARATION

Iron fallout removal, tar treatment, and panel cleansing reset the paint for perfect bonding.

Iron FalloutTar RemovalPanel Prep
Stage 04

COMPOUNDING, POLISHING, AND IPA

Multi-stage correction refines gloss, then an IPA wipe-down reveals pure, inspection-ready clarity.

CorrectionGlossIPA Wipe
Stage 05

EXTERIOR PROTECTION APPLICATION

Ceramic coating or sealant is applied panel by panel to lock in gloss and hydrophobic protection.

CeramicSealantHydrophobic
Stage 06

GLASS COATING

Glass is polished, de-greased, and coated for clarity, safer wet-weather visibility, and easy cleaning.

ClaritySafety
Stage 07

INTERIOR COATING AND SANITISATION

Steam, fabric treatment, leather care, and antimicrobial protection elevate the cabin.

SteamLeather CareSanitise
Stage 08

PRODUCT CURING AND ACCESSORIES INTEGRATION

Coatings cure under controlled heat while trim, tires, badges, and accessories are finished.

CureTrim FinishAccessories
Stage 09

QUALITY INSPECTION AND HANDOVER

High-intensity lighting confirms flawless results before final photography and handover.

InspectionPhotographyHandover

Our Story

We Are
DDS

Born in the desert, Built for shine. Desert Detailing Studio was built on a single conviction: every car deserves to look extraordinary, even under Jaipur heat and dust. Our single workshop blends premium materials, skilled hands, and strict inspection standards to deliver real, lasting shine.

We specialize in PPF, paint correction, ceramic coatings, interior restoration, and full detailing packages. Every vehicle follows a staged process designed to reveal defects, correct them, and lock in protection.

Whether it is a daily driver or a weekend machine, we build the plan around your car, your climate, and your finish goals.
